Speak for Me is designed with privacy and compliance in mind. This page covers data handling, GDPR compliance, data export, and account deletion.

## Data We Collect

Speak for Me processes the following data:

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Slack messages** | Used to generate contextual suggestions | Not stored permanently; processed in real-time |
| **Style preferences** | Tone, formality, preferred/avoided phrases | Stored until you delete them |
| **Person context** | Notes about people you interact with | Stored until you delete them |
| **Conversation context** | Notes about specific channels or DMs | Stored until you delete them |
| **Suggestion feedback** | Whether you accepted, refined, or dismissed a suggestion | Stored for improving quality |
| **Usage records** | Suggestion counts per billing period | Stored for billing purposes |
| **Audit logs** | Security events (login, data export, settings changes) | Retained for 90 days |

<Callout type="info">
  Slack message content is processed in real-time by the AI and is **not** stored in our database. Only metadata (timestamps, channel IDs) is retained for suggestion tracking.
</Callout>

## GDPR Compliance

Speak for Me complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Consent**: You must explicitly consent to data processing when you first authorize the app
- **Right to Access**: You can export all your personal data at any time
- **Right to Erasure**: You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Data Portability**: Exported data is provided in a standard JSON format
- **Data Minimization**: We only collect data necessary for the service to function

## Exporting Your Data

You can download a complete copy of all your personal data.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Privacy Settings

    Sign in to the [dashboard](/dashboard), expand the **Account** group, select **Settings**, then navigate to **Privacy & Data**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Request Data Export

    Click the **Export My Data** button. The system will compile all your data including style preferences, person context, conversation context, suggestion history, and usage records.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Download the Export

    Once the export is ready (usually within a few minutes), you will receive a download link. The data is provided as a JSON file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Data exports include all information associated with your Slack user ID across all connected workspaces. The export is generated as a secure download link valid for 24 hours.
</Callout>

## Deleting Your Account

You have the right to request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Privacy Settings

    Sign in to the [dashboard](/dashboard), expand the **Account** group, select **Settings**, then navigate to **Privacy & Data**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Request Account Deletion

    Click the **Delete My Account** button. You will be asked to confirm this action.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm Deletion

    After confirmation, your account enters a 30-day grace period. During this time you can cancel the deletion by signing back in. After 30 days, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
  Account deletion is permanent after the 30-day grace period. This removes all your style preferences, person context, conversation context, suggestion history, and usage data. This action cannot be undone.
</Callout>

You can manage cookie preferences through the consent banner that appears on your first visit.

## Audit Logging

Speak for Me maintains audit logs for security-relevant events:

| Event | What is Logged |
|-------|---------------|
| **Login / Logout** | Timestamp, IP address, user agent |
| **Data Export** | When requested and when completed |
| **Account Deletion** | When requested and when executed |
| **Subscription Changes** | Plan upgrades, downgrades, cancellations |
| **Settings Changes** | Style preference updates, context changes |
| **OAuth Connections** | Google Sheets connected or disconnected |
| **Admin Actions** | Role changes, organization settings updates |
| **Guardrail Violations** | Blocked or flagged suggestion content |
| **Template Changes** | Template submissions, approvals, rejections |

Audit log retention depends on your plan tier (7 days for Free, 30 days for Starter, 90 days for Pro and above). See the [Audit Trail](/docs/admin/audit-trail) page for details on filtering, exporting, and data retention.
