This guide walks you through every step to get the most out of Speak for Me. Follow along to configure your workspace, personalize your experience, and unlock all available features.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Install the Slack app

    Visit the [homepage](/) and click **Add to Slack**. Authorize the requested permissions to add Speak for Me to your workspace.

    See the [installation guide](/docs/getting-started/installation) for full details.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Sign in to the web portal

    After installation, sign in to the [web portal dashboard](/dashboard) using your Slack account. The dashboard is where you manage settings, view history, and configure preferences.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Configure your style preferences

    Open the **Style Settings** page from the dashboard sidebar. Set your preferred tone, formality level, and any custom phrases you want the AI to use or avoid.

    <Callout type="warn">
      Style changes take effect on your next suggestion. Existing suggestions will not be regenerated.
    </Callout>

    See [Style Settings](/docs/features/style-settings) for more details.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Start watching your first conversation

    In any Slack channel, type `/speakforme-watch` to begin monitoring. Speak for Me will start generating suggestions when relevant messages appear.

    See [Watching Conversations](/docs/features/watching) for details on what types of messages trigger suggestions.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Get and refine your first suggestion

    Wait for a message in your watched channel, then review the AI suggestion. Click **Refine** to adjust it with natural language feedback like "make it shorter" or "more formal."

    See [Refining Suggestions](/docs/features/refinement) for tips on effective refinement.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### (Optional) Set up weekly reports

    If your team uses Slack Workflow Builder for status updates, you can configure automated weekly reports. Connect Google Sheets and set up a reporting schedule.

    See [Weekly Reports](/docs/features/reports) for the full setup walkthrough.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### (Optional) Connect Google Sheets

    For report data storage and sharing, connect your Google account from the dashboard. This enables automatic spreadsheet updates and scheduled report generation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## You are all set

Once you have completed these steps, Speak for Me will work in the background to help you respond to messages more effectively. The more you use it, the better it gets at matching your style.

Explore the [Features](/docs/features) section to learn about everything Speak for Me can do.
